What we do ?
Coders Today is an initiative in the form of a women’s coding club to bridge the gap between our education system and corporate world.

We encourage women into coding and related fields by providing experiential learning, make women employable and entrepreneurial and thus giving space to women’s perspective in the corporate world which can revolutionize businesses.
Our Mentors
Our mentors guide students in creating projects and thus make them skilled in their respective areas.
All of our mentors are working professionals and experienced in their fields.
